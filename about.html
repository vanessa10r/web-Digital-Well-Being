Project Presentation

This project aims to help users better understand the digital world, technological tools, and practical strategies for using technology responsibly in everyday life.

Objective of the Resource

The main objective is to provide clear concepts and useful recommendations that guide users toward responsible digital behavior and a healthier relationship with technology.

Target Audience

This resource is designed especially for university students who want to improve their digital wellbeing and develop greater awareness about the use of technology.

How to Use the Application

Users can explore different sections of the application to learn key concepts, discover practical tips, and reflect on their own digital habits.
